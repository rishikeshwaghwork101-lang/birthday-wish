Happy Birthday Girl, Persia. ❤️

I'm really glad that I get to be a small piece of your big life.

You know, one of the things I admire most about you is that you were always up for helping me. You're amazing just being yourself, whether you're being expressive, writing poetry, laughing at the most lame jokes, being completely chaotic, or being brutally honest. There is something refreshing about people who don't spend their lives trying to become someone else. You're one of those people.

You have this beautiful heart, it's rare, really rare in people nowadays. I genuinely hope you never lose that.

I hope you keep writing poems, even the ones you think aren't good enough. I hope you keep reading books that make you think. I hope you keep questioning things, feeling deeply, and speaking your mind the way you always do.

You have this habit of making people comfortable enough to be themselves. It's one of your biggest strengths. Conversations with you rarely stay superficial. They somehow end up becoming meaningful, and I think that's a gift.

Thank you for every conversation we've had, whether it was about books, poetry, life, random nonsense, or just making each other laugh. Thank you for your honesty, even when it wasn't the easiest thing to say. Thank you for believing in people you care about and encouraging them to become better versions of themselves.

You've reminded me more than once that not everyone has an ulterior motive to be kind to others. Sometimes it just needs someone who believes you'll get there.

You're genuinely a wonderful friend, and I hope you know how valuable that is. People often underestimate how much one good friend can change someone's life. Whether you realise it or not, you've made a meaningful difference in mine.

I hope this year gives you everything you deserve—peace when you need it, adventures when you're ready for them, stories worth writing, books worth remembering, people who value your heart, and countless reasons to smile, giggle, and laugh like maniacs.

Keep being the wonderfully weird, thoughtful, expressive, kind-hearted, and beautifully imperfect person you are. That's the version of you the world needs.

Happy Birthday once again, Persia. May this year be kinder to you than the last, and may you always have the courage to remain exactly who you are.

With lots of love and good wishes,

Rishi..
